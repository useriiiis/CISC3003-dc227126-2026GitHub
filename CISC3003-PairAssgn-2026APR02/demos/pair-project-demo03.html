About Our Café

Pair04 Coffee House is a demo website created to present a modern café brand with a clean visual style, section-based layout, and responsive browsing experience.

This design focuses on menu presentation, consistent spacing, accessible navigation, and card-based layout for mobile, tablet, and desktop users.
