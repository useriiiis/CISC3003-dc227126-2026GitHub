The principles that guide my work and life

Innovation

Technology evolves rapidly, and I strive to stay at the forefront. Whether developing new web solutions or exploring security techniques, I embrace creative problem-solving and cutting-edge approaches.

🤝

Collaboration

My CTF experience taught me that teamwork achieves more than individual effort. I value diverse perspectives, open communication, and building solutions together with peers and mentors.

📚

Continuous Learning

Computer science and cybersecurity demand lifelong learning. I dedicate time to reading, practicing, and staying current with industry trends, certifications, and emerging threats.

💡

Social Responsibility

My Career Aspirations

Where I see myself in the technology industry

Building a Future in Technology

As a Computer Science student at the University of Macau with a passion for cybersecurity and web development, I envision a career that combines technical expertise with meaningful impact. My NewStar CTF 2nd Place achievement reinforced my commitment to security, and I aim to grow into roles that protect organizations and users from digital threats.

Short-term Goals (1–2 years)

  • Graduate with honors from the University of Macau
  • Obtain CompTIA Security+ and CEH certifications
  • Secure an internship or entry-level role in cybersecurity
  • Build a strong portfolio of web and security projects
  • Contribute to open-source security tools

Long-term Goals (3–5 years)

  • Work as a Security Engineer or Penetration Tester
  • Pursue advanced certifications (CISSP, OSCP)
  • Specialize in web application security
  • Mentor students and junior professionals
  • Speak at security conferences and workshops
